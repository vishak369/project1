About us

Vibhavam pursues excellence offering delightful, honest and reliable event managaement & services in Thrissur,kerala. We have been started more than 15 years & continue to fulfill the desire of our happy customers. We engage in providing excellent catering services to our clients, we can customize our dishes & menu to suit your individual taste, budget and the type of occasion. Vibhavam accepts various events like Marriage, Reception, Birthday, Anniversaries, House warming, Parties and all other occasions. We provide home made high quality spices and all our products will be delivered to your doorstep. Vibhavam makes your occasion very special and memorable with our excellent service.
